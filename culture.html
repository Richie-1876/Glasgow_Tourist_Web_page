Live Music

Glasgow has many great venues, big and small, across the city. From the Armadillo to the Barrowland Ballroom and King Tuts Wah Wah Hut. Be a part of the rich Glasgow music scene and support local artists.

Museums

World-famous museums are found all over the city and beyond. Covering things like art, architecture, and history. A short drive from the city is Stirling which boasts the Wallace Monument and the Battle of Bannockburn visitor center.

City of Culture

Home of the World Pipe Band championships Glasgow boasts similar events year-round including the Highland Games. Winner of European City of Culture in 1990, there is plenty to soak up
