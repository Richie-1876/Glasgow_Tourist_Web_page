Food & Drink

Food and drink in the city spans many cuisines, with great restaurants catering for your every desire. But why not sample some of the local delicacies including Irn Bru, Haggis neeps and Tatties, Tattie Scones, Tunnocks Teacakes, and Scotch Whiskey. And of course, if you are brave enough, why not give a deep-fried Mars bar a try, you may be pleasantly surprised.
